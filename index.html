About me

Hello, I’m Katharine Wilson, a journalist in the Washington, D.C. metro area. I am passionate about delivering political and local news in a helpful, human-focused way.

This fall, I’m covering Congress and the Supreme Court for Capital News Service. I’ll also be writing investigative and feature stories for The Diamondback as a senior staff writer.

This summer, I was in New York City interning for the MSNBC news show Alex Reports and contributing to NBC News Digital. In August, I went to Chicago to cover the Democratic National Convention for Capital News Service.

I have also reported on state and local news for The Diamondback, The Herald-Mail, Montgomery Community Media and College Park Here & Now.

I specialize in writing features, investigations, and breaking news stories with a politics focus. I covered city, county, state and national politics over the last three years. As a local news reporter, I have covered a wide range of issues including businesses, gentrification, education, and the criminal justice system.

Outside of politics and general news reporting, I am passionate about arts and entertainment reporting. I produce a biweekly pop culture news show for WMUC-FM radio and have written extensive features about local artists for various publications.

After I graduate from the University of Maryland in May 2025, I plan to spend my career writing about the intersections of policy and people.
